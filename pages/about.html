About

Peace!

Photography has served me better than I expected it to when I first began taking photos. It functions as a kind of handshake at times; other times it functions more as a window into the lives of people I meet. I still can’t decide if I prefer taking candid street photos or portraits; what I do know is shooting on film has given me even more opportunities to practice mindfulness as each shot I take is intended to be shot with intention.

My first camera, which is the only one I have and use to this day, is a Canon A1 35mm. If you’re interested in taking some pictures or collaborating, feel free to contact me!
