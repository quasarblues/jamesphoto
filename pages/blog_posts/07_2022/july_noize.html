July 2022

~Dreamscapes~

Saunter. Wayfaring. Journey. A vagrant somnambulating. An enigma disclosed proposed I wake, catch my dreams swimming in the gloaming, glowing in the current moment. Sleep beckons one to awaken.

An escape from the day, from work. Pour daydreams and dreamscapes. Scraping solace from the day with a method tried and true.

Words of black ink reveal a night sky scratching through the white page. Constellations of the mind’s shape guide me through my own thoughts, through my own heart. A journal is the journey in shapes and symbols.

Amidst a steady four count of the moving Seoul. Sirens sailing. Footsteps stomping on sweltering pavement. Jack hammers destroying structures, exposing the Earth beneath—stop. Rumination in your temple. About what, i’m curious. Perhaps, just rest. A breath in the freedom of self. A brief moment dipped in isolation. Away from the crowd, away from others. Looking within and without to simply be.
