September 2022

Pain as seen through Panes

A reflective circlet with a lambent gloaming
just past noon. On further inspection, a besmudged mirror.
Tiny iridules painting parhelia in my heart.
Light frozen in crystals floating in a
tawny-splashed bath of time.
A reflection dependent upon your vantage point.

Framed city escapes through the trace of memory.
One summer day.
Arrière penseé.
The heart astir amongst friends.
Windows to the Seoul.
Yet, the soul blurs time and space as
both begin to trace what once was.

A vestige of moments-had
evaporate into a web of
cirrostratus memories shape shifting into the azure.
Blue melodies
I sing in the absence of colo(u)r.
My soul ensconced in Jazz chords and smoky records,
plumes of thought like gossamer drape my mind.
Shadows behind the glass
dance on two planes which
shade a moment.
Passing to and from,
cascading into otherness, irrelevant
because only "here and now"
matter.

Fey blues in episodes, frame by frame,
glow past as we disintegrate into stillicides.
Spectrums refract light
passing through prisms in our eyes,
prisons in our minds.
Indelibly inscribed, the words we spoke--reverberating,
illuminating a path trodden with heavy heart.
Lemniscates weaving infinite blues like>
fingerprints of the universe reminding me
whence the poet and the romantic orb meaning.
Put simply,
I'll find you again in rich caesura,
echoing the moment
love alighted.
