qNtM Noize

November 2022

Your Love Reverberates

Four strings on a crimson bass,
black lemniscates stretch into the universe—
you taught me how to feel rhythm,
balance the harmony,
don’t lose the beat,
tune it or die.

The harmony found between souls—mediums of music—
exchange grief, sorrow, pain, love and joy,
kindled through instruments.
You taught me how to listen.

I hiked a mountain to watch the sun
rise above horizons glowing in the
heart of winter,
dawn's own music serenading in the bosom of
mourning,
above the blankets powdering cerulean
ridges, azure gradients echoing
yesterday and every memory I have of
you playing bass—
release.

Inly I listen to silence anew,
gradients of experience incise and weave through the
filigree of the firmament—
your heartbeat magnified in a new way of listening.
Your form a versipellous butterfly—each flutter of the wings
painting golden, the sky a deep amber.

I hold your gaze a moment and a moment only—until the next,
the tendrils of time tower aloft, your supernal glow warms my heart.
icy blankets rest on the mountain—
golden azure kissing the ridges,
orbs of recueillement and prayer,
I continuously find you,
until we find each other,
again.
