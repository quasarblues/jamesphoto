Seoul to Busan: Fixed Gear

For several years I had heard of and had planned to do the bike ride from Seoul to Busan. I had used cycling as a means to explore the city of Seoul since I had lived there and this route was a way for me to expand the parameters of my exploration. It took me six days. I had plenty of time to complete the ride so for me it was more about enjoying the journey and getting to know the countryside of Korea as seen on a bicycle. Seoul is very unusual in comparison to the rest of the country; the photos can only express a small amount of how serene this country is. The people—even in the midst of a pandemic—were so friendly, open and caring. Riding this route by myself was a special experience and one that I’ll always cherish.
