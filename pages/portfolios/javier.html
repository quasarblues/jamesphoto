Javier Collab

This collaboration was one of the most meaningful things I have done with my photography. Javier and I had become friends the previous year and as their date to leave the country approached we began to spend a lot more time together. We planned to do a shoot and had brought in another photographer/videographer, Senedra, to join us. We went to an island in Incheon and spent the day there. The way the day evolved, everything began to seem more like a narrative writing its own story. I am going to forever be thankful for this shoot.
